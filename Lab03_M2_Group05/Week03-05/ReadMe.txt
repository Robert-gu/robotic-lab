Key.up Key.down Key.left Key.right move the robot forward and backward or turn it right and left.
Due to friction please don't turn the robot left or right for too long or too short. Pressing about half a second is best.
The robot only take one command at a time. e.g. it can't go forward and turn left at the same time.
